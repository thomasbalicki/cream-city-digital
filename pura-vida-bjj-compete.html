Programs

COMPETITION PROGRAMS

Two dedicated programs for competitive athletes — one focused on the gi, one on the cage. Both built around the same core: hard work, coachable mindset, and a team that fights for each other.

BJJ Competition
BJJ COMPETITION TEAM

Led by Courtnie Korpela — the 4th woman in Wisconsin to earn a BJJ black belt under the legendary Master Pedro Sauer — the Pura Vida BJJ Competition Team is open to all skill levels and ages. Courtnie brings 18+ years of competing and coaching experience at the highest levels of the sport, giving our competitors access to world-class instruction on both the competitive and technical sides of jiu-jitsu.

What you get
Dedicated competition drilling sessions
Strategy and game-plan coaching per tournament
Team corner support at local and national events
Access to elite training partners daily
Head Coach
Courtnie Korpela
1st Degree BJJ Black Belt · Pedro Sauer lineage
MMA Fight Team
MMA FIGHT TEAM

Pura Vida's MMA Fight Team is where serious fighters prepare to compete professionally. Training daily alongside UFC-level athletes, the team benefits from an environment built on real competition pressure. Our coaching staff — which includes active and former UFC fighters — understands exactly what it takes to develop a complete, cage-ready competitor.

What you get
Full MMA preparation — striking, wrestling, BJJ, conditioning
Fight-night cornering by experienced coaches
Access to fight camps and sparring with pro partners
Matchmaking support and promotion relationships
Head Coaches
Zak Ottow & Jake Klipp
Former UFC Fighter · BJJ Black Belt · USMC Veteran
