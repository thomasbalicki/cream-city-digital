About Me

Why I started
Cream City Digital.

I started Cream City Digital because I believe professional web design should be accessible to every business — not just companies with large marketing budgets.

Building a high-quality website is faster and more efficient than it used to be, but many agencies still charge prices that put great web design out of reach for small businesses. My goal is to change that by delivering modern, custom websites that look professional, perform well, and are priced fairly.

"Based in Milwaukee, I work with small businesses throughout Wisconsin and across the United States to create websites that build trust, showcase their work, and help generate new customers."

My background combines business, marketing, and technology, with experience in full-stack web development and data engineering. That mix allows me to look beyond the design itself and focus on what actually makes a website successful. A great website should be fast, mobile-friendly, easy to navigate, and built with SEO best practices so potential customers can find your business online.

I also understand that most business owners aren't looking for another project to manage. You're busy running your business, serving customers, and growing your company — not learning web development or spending hours updating your website. That's why I build websites that are easy to maintain, reliable, and designed to keep working for your business long after they're launched.

Whether you need a brand-new website or want to modernize an outdated one, my goal is the same: create a website that reflects the quality of your business. Every project is built with a clean, professional design, clear messaging, and a focus on turning visitors into customers.

At the end of the day, I believe good web design isn't about flashy animations or unnecessary complexity. It's about creating a website that makes a great first impression, earns trust, and helps your business grow.
